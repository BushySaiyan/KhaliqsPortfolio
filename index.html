About

I am currently finishing my Computer Science degree, where I've developed a deep passion for programming and software development. Although my initial interests were in art and design, I gradually discovered the creativity and problem-solving aspects of programming. This shift sparked my love for technology and fueled my desire to pursue a career in this field.

Originally from Pakistan, I spent over 15 years living in Malaysia, where I attended an international school. The experience of interacting with people from diverse cultural backgrounds broadened my horizons and taught me the value of understanding different perspectives.

In my earlier years, I was deeply involved in sports such as football, sprinting, long-distance running, and swimming. I won numerous medals in these activities, but an unexpected injury forced me to reconsider my path. This led me to explore the world of video games, where I pursued a professional career in Dota 2 and won the South East Asia World Cup in 2016.

Despite my success in esports, I eventually realized that a sustainable future lay in my education. I shifted my focus back to academics, which has led me to where I am today—dedicated to completing my degree and pursuing a career in technology.
